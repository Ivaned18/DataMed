Welcome to DataMed Web Application.

DataMed is application for hospital where the doctor can get the basic information about his patient.

Why use DataMed?

When a patient have a emergency, the doctor have to do a analisys but don't have any time for do it. Now we have a solution for this case, now only with a token the doctor can meet the patient about basic information...
